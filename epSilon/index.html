WHO WE ARE

Epsilon is a freelance game development company created by independent game developers from various parts of the world. Our team is growing and developing day by day.

We are a company that produces various platform games, fps games and online games. In addition, we strive to meet the requirements of the industry by improving ourselves day by day.
